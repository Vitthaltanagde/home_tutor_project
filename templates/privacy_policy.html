Privacy Policy

At GuruGO, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our tutor-finding services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your name (student or parent)
  • Contact information (phone number, email)
  • Address and location (including city and pincode)
  • Education details (class, board, mode of tuition)
  • Technical data like IP address, device type, browser

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Match you with suitable home/private tutors
  • Send demo class confirmations and reminders
  • Improve our website and services
  • Respond to your queries or support requests

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with verified tutors solely for arranging demo classes. All tutors are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies to enhance your experience. These cookies track user behavior but do not store personal information.

5. Data Security

Your data is stored securely in our encrypted database and is accessible only by authorized admin personnel. We use HTTPS encryption for all transactions.

6. Your Rights

You can contact us anytime to:

  • Request deletion or correction of your data
  • Opt-out of promotional messages
  • Request a copy of your stored information

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
