Top 7 Tips to Choose the Right Tutor for Your Child

Finding the right tutor can make all the difference in your child’s academic success. Here are 7 practical tips to help you make the best choice:

  1. Identify your child’s needs and weak subjects
  2. Look for qualifications and teaching experience
  3. Check compatibility through a demo class
  4. Ask for reviews or feedback from previous students

  6. Discuss teaching style and progress tracking
